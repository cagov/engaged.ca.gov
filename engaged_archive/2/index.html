Welcome

The agenda setting phase of the Los Angeles wildfires recovery engagement has closed. Visit Engaged California to learn more and to sign up to join a future engagement.

Tell us your priorities for the LA fires recovery. We’ll use your priorities to set topics for the next steps in community decision-making.

The State of California, LA County, and the City of LA want to hear your thoughts on the most pressing topics for recovery and rebuilding after the LA fires.

Your input will help identify the most important topics for community discussions on wildfire recovery and rebuilding in the affected communities in LA County and City.

🕒 Estimated time to complete: 15 minutes or less

expand_less expand_more How to participate

You will answer questions to identify what community recovery topics need attention. Your responses will help determine where to focus deeper discussion and engagement.

Click Next to move through the pages. Your first task is to review the Civility pledge and Moderation policy. After you accept the pledge and moderation policy, you will answer questions and vote on your top priorities.
