Civility pledge

The agenda setting phase of the Los Angeles wildfires recovery engagement has closed. Visit Engaged California to learn more and to sign up to join a future engagement.

We want this platform to be a safe space for everyone who participates. When posting comments, make sure your words are appropriate for everyone.

Our civility principles and other rules are in place to ensure we respect one another. You must review and accept them before you can proceed.

Civility principles

As a participant you agree to respect and adhere to these principles while on this site:

  1. Be authentic online. I will represent myself truthfully. I will not pretend to be someone else.
  2. Communicate my intentions clearly and respectfully. I will be open about my goals, use polite language, and not use profanity or hateful speech.
  3. Respect privacy and confidentiality. I will not share private or personal information about others. I will remember my contributions may become part of a permanent public record.
  4. Be transparent about my interests and affiliations. I will disclose any personal or financial stakes in discussions.
  5. Be truthful and address my mistakes promptly. I will strive to be truthful. If I make an error, I will address it to the best of my ability.
