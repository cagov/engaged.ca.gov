    1. Empower Citizens Through Outside-In Design Problem: Government services are designed inside-out, around agency processes rather than citizen needs. This leads to frustration, inefficiency, and loss of trust. Recommendation: Apply an outside-in design approach, mapping services around resident life events. Projects often stop evolving once deployed, locking in inefficiency. Recommendation: Require statutory/regulatory review on a recurring cycle to keep policies modern. Build agility clauses into law to allow agencies to update service models without full legislative resets.
