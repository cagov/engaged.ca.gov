Tell us how supportive you are of the following recovery efforts in this category. Rate each option on a 7-point scale.

This exercise has 2 goals. The first is to generate a list of your top-rated options. The second is to highlight the recovery options that have community consensus.

Your responses are automatically saved. You can finish your responses later if needed.

The following resources can inform you as you weigh these options.
