Moderation policy

expand_less expand_more Comment guidelines

The agenda setting phase of the Los Angeles wildfires recovery engagement has closed. Visit Engaged California to learn more and to sign up to join a future engagement.

Effective March 19, 2025

Comment guidelines

Before posting a comment, please keep in mind:

  • We review comments after they are posted.
  • We expect everyone to be respectful and considerate when commenting.

Comments will be removed if they:

  • Include personal attacks against the public or specific individuals.
  • Promote or advertise services or products without authorization.
  • Contain abusive, profane, or vulgar language.
  • Contain sexual content, overly graphic, disturbing, obscene or offensive material, or material that would otherwise violate the law if published on this site.
  • Use offensive language that targets specific ethnic, religious, or racial groups.
  • Include embedding URLs, hyperlinks, or references to external websites, including link shorteners.

We do not edit public comments. If your comment contains inappropriate content, it will be removed. We do not remove comments critical of government or public officials if they are otherwise compliant with the comment guidelines.

Comment review and posting schedule

  • Comments are welcome 24/7.
  • Moderation happens during business hours.
  • Comments submitted after hours, on weekends, or on holidays will be reviewed and posted the next business day if they meet these guidelines.

Privacy protection

To protect your privacy and the privacy of others:

  • Do not include personal information (full name, Social Security number, phone numbers, addresses, or email addresses) in comments.
  • Comments containing personal information may not be posted.
  • We will never request personal information via Ethelo comments, including on the official Engaged California website or from the Engaged California email account.

Endorsement policy

  • Any mentions of commercial products, services, or organizations are for informational purposes only.
  • These mentions do not indicate an official endorsement by the State of California.

Public records and contact information

  • Comments shared on this platform may be subject to public disclosure under the Public Records Act (PRA).
  • Comments in Ethelo do not serve as official communication with the State of California.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated or amended at any time. Please check back for any changes.
