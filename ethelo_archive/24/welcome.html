Welcome

The state employees engagement has closed. Visit Engaged California for more information.

Share your ideas to improve California government

We’re asking for your insights, first and foremost, as a Californian. As a state employee, you bring a unique perspective and are an expert on how our government serves its residents. We value your experience from both sides: as a state employee, and as a Californian using public services.

Your perspective matters.

We have 2 goals for this work:

  • Find challenges that undermine our ability to work efficiently and effectively
  • Discover ways to better serve the people in our state
     

🕒 Estimated time: 15 minutes or less

In addition to sharing your own responses, you can review and discuss your colleagues’ ideas. We encourage you to read their ideas, like ideas that you support, leave comments, or just explore. You can participate in that discussion now, or come back later.

This platform is exclusively for State of California employees. If you are not a state employee, please do not engage with this platform.

We take your privacy seriously. We safeguard the privacy of personal information we collect or maintain.
