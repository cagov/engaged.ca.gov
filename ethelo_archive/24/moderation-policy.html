Moderation policy

expand_less expand_more Comment guidelines

The state employees engagement has closed. Visit Engaged California for more information.

Effective August 15, 2025

Comment guidelines

Before posting a comment, please keep in mind:

  • We review comment submissions before they are posted for others to see
  • We expect everyone to be respectful and considerate when commenting
  • Comments should be original and authentic to the person leaving them
  • We do not remove comments critical of government or public officials if they are otherwise compliant with the comment guidelines

Comments will be removed if they:

  • Include personal attacks against the public or specific individuals
  • Promote or advertise services or products without authorization
  • Contain abusive, profane, or vulgar language
  • Contain sexual content, overly graphic, disturbing, obscene or offensive material, or material that would otherwise violate the law if published on this site
  • Use offensive language that targets specific ethnic, religious, or racial groups
  • Include embedding URLs, hyperlinks, or references to external websites, including link shorteners

We do not edit public comments. If your comment contains inappropriate content, it will be removed.

Comment review and posting schedule

Comments are welcome 24/7 but moderation happens during business hours.

Comments submitted after hours, on weekends, or on holidays will be reviewed and posted the next business day if they meet these guidelines.

Privacy protection

To protect your privacy and the privacy of others:

  • Do not include personally identifiable information (PII) (full name, Social Security number, phone numbers, addresses, or email addresses) in comments
  • We will never request PII via Ethelo comments, including on the official Engaged California website or from the Engaged California email account

Endorsement policy

Any mentions of commercial products, services, or organizations are for informational purposes only. These mentions do not indicate an official endorsement by the State of California.

Public records and contact information

  • Comments shared on this platform may be subject to public disclosure under the Public Records Act
  • Comments in Ethelo do not serve as official communication with the State of California

Policy updates

This policy may be updated or amended at any time. Please check back for any changes.
